About

I am an east coast based creative services manager with a background in web design and development. In addition, I like to read books, write, and dabble in visual mediums including photography and painting. My background in journalism gave me the tools to write for an audience. I like to say that my MA in Liberal Studies deepened my ability to navigate ambiguity. Since going back to graduate school wasn't an option --cough cough, student loans-- I found ways to deepen my education in design, coding languages and web production through organizations like Girl Develop It!, General Assembly, Fleischer Art Memorial and other great resources online.


If you're interested in collaborating with me on a project, feel free to email me at: martineaukt@gmail.com
