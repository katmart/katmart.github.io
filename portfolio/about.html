I am an east-coast based creative type with a background in front-end web design/development and print production. In addition, I like to read books, write, and dabble in visual mediums including photography and painting. My background in journalism gave me the tools to write for an audience. I like to say that my MA in Liberal Studies deepened my ability to navigate ambiguity. My experience at Creative Circle also gives me significant client management and recruiting expeirence. This enables me to discuss project scopes and stick to tight deadlines, and this will translate well into any creative environment. As a self-proclaimed autodidact, I found ways to deepen my education in design, coding languages and web production through organizations like Girl Develop It!, General Assembly, Fleischer Art Memorial and other great resources online.


I also coded this site in Sublime text with HTML5 and CSS3. jQuery/Javascript functionality will come your way soon!
