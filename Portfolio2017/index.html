Kat Martineau started off as a jill of all trades with website production, recruitment, project management, customer service, and event planning. This morphed into independent design and technology studies with General Assembly, Girl Develop It!, and many other web based resources and conferences. All of this experience has morphed into a toolbox that lends itself well to user experience design. Now, Kat focuses heavily on usability testing, content migration, content writing, and website production at an association management software company in the Baltimore area. She also performs improvisational comedy with Baltimore Improv Group and Charm City Comedy Project. She sits on BIG's board and recently redesigned their website.



I coded this site in Sublime text with HTML5 and CSS3.
