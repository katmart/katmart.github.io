About Me

Currently I am a recruiter for creative and technology professionals in Philadelphia, and I have worked on deepening my web design and development knowledge since 2010. Previous to recruiting, I worked as a freelance production artist for print and digital projets in the Atlanta area and as a marketing coordinator for education non-profit organizations in New York City. I started my coding journey when I had to change e-mail marketing templates for one of my non-profit positions.

Since I moved to Philadelphia, I have taken courses with Girl Develop It! and Fleischer Art Memorial to deepen my coding and fine arts knowledge.
