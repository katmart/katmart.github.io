Form Builder

Content > Page Manager > Form Builder


StarChapter allows chapter leaders to build forms via the admin area. It is easy to publish the form to the website or send it via email to both members and prospects. This powerful feature allows for seamless data integration to meeting and membership areas. With this tool, chapter leaders can capture information about their member base and access downloadable documents of responses.

  • Job Listing - Description and Initial Setup

    What is a Job Listing?

    Professional association websites are a hub for highly talented and motivated professionals who are looking for work in a specialized field. By targeting members and prospects alike, posting jobs can connect an association with potential sponsors and partnerships. In addition, there is the capacity to generate revenue from job listings.


    What is the first step?

    Posting a job requires three parts of the admin area: Form Builder, Classifieds and Navigation Manager.


    Once you identify those thre areas, start by going to Form Builder > Add a Form.
